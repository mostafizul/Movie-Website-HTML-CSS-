Company Overview

Our Story

Moviesworld.com is a Bangladeshi media-services provider and production company headquartered in Dhaka, Bangladesh, founded in 2020 by Karim Techno Ltd. The company's primary business is its subscription-based streaming service which offers online streaming of a library of films and television programs, including those produced in-house. Moviesworld.com had over 148 million paid subscriptions worldwide, including 60 million in the Bangladesh, India & Pakistan and over 154 million subscriptions total including free trials.
